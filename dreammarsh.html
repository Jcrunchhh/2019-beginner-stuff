Ionah-Cho Sim Date

Ionah-Cho Sim Date is a dating simulation game for girls. You play as a teenaged girl attending Ionah High School as a first year. You get 30 days to build your relationship with 6 dateable guys in this town, work to earn money, buy gifts, and go on dates.


There are 7 different endings.


Game is in progress!
